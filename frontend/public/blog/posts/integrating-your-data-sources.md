# Integrating Your Data Sources

One of the most powerful features of Revtops is the ability to connect all your revenue data sources in one place. This guide will walk you through the integration process.

## Supported Integrations

Revtops currently supports integrations with popular platforms including:

- **CRM Systems**: Salesforce, HubSpot
- **Messaging**: Slack, Outlook Mail, Google Mail
- **Calendars**: Outlook Calendar, Google Calendar
- And more every day. We'll update the above list as we go along.

## Quick Setup Process

### Step 1: Navigate to Data Sources

Click on the "Data Sources" tab in the sidebar to view all available integrations.

### Step 2: Connect Your Account

For each integration you want to add:

1. Click the "Connect" button
2. Authenticate with your account
3. Grant the necessary permissions
4. Wait for the initial sync to complete

### Step 3: Start Asking Questions

Once your data sources are connected, you can immediately start asking questions like:

> "What were our top performing campaigns last quarter?"

> "Show me deals that closed in the last 30 days"

> "Which support tickets are taking the longest to resolve?"

## Benefits of Integration

By connecting your data sources, you unlock:

- **Unified View**: See all your revenue data in one place
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Ask questions in natural language
- **Real-time Updates**: Your data stays fresh and accurate
- **Cross-platform Analysis**: Identify trends across different tools

## Best Practices

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your integrations:

1. **Connect all relevant sources**: The more data you connect, the better insights you'll get
3. **Monitor sync status**: Check the Data Sources page regularly to ensure everything is syncing properly
4. **Start with questions**: Don't just connect and forget - actively use your data!

## Troubleshooting

Having issues with an integration? Check out these common solutions:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Sync failing | Re-authenticate and check permissions |
| Missing data | Verify the data exists in the source system |
| Slow performance | Large datasets may take time on initial sync |
